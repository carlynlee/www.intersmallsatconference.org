Conference Registration

Thank you for registering to attend the 2014 Interplanetary Small Satellite Conference. All events (including dinner) will be held at Caltech's Beckman Institute.

Your registration includes dinner on Monday night. Please email registration@intersmallsatconference.org if you have any dietary restrictions.

Four registration options are offered, in addition to guest tickets for the dinner:

  • $50 Student Registration*
  • $25 Student One-Day Registation*
  • $175 Professional Registration**
  • $85 Professional One-Day Registration**
  • $20 Guest Dinner Ticket

Note: Registration refunds will no longer be possible after April 9, 2014.

*Those paying the student fee must be full-time registered graduate or undergraduate students and will be asked to show valid student ID during conference check-in.

**JPL and NASA employees who are funded to attend through their organization should not register on this web page. Instead, email registration@intersmallsatconference.org to arrange payment via direct transfer.







Funds are processed by Authorize.net on behalf of Caltech, and the transaction will appear as "Caltech-Interplanetary Small Satellite" on your credit card statement. You will also receive an email invoice with this information.
