Past Conferences: 2013


20-21 June 2013

California Institute of Technology

Pasadena, California


The 1st Interplanetary Small Satellite Conference was held at the California Institute of Technology on 20-21 June, 2013. The conference was organized by a group of students, alumni, and staff from Caltech, MIT, Cornell, the University of Michigan, and JPL. We are grateful to all those who helped make it such a success.

Speakers


We are grateful to our 2013 keynote speakers Simon (Pete) Worden, Center Director for NASA Ames, and Geoff Marcy, Professor of Astronomy at the University of California at Berkeley and visionary exoplanet hunter. Dr. Worden and Dr. Marcy shared their thoughts exploration and the unique challenges and opportunities presented by interplanetary small satellites.
